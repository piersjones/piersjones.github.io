Pradaxa®: The only NOAC with immediate
reversal in rare emergency situations1–5

Praxbind® is a specific reversal agent for dabigatran and is indicated in adult patients treated
with Pradaxa® (dabigatran etexilate) when rapid reversal of its anticoagulant effects is required:1

• For emergency surgery/urgent procedures• In life-threatening or uncontrolled bleeding
